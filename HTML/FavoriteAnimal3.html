Greg
Gregory the Elephant

Greg is from the Cartoon Network show Over the Garden Wall, and he is the younger of the two main characters. He wears a pot on his head in order to look like an elephant, because that is his halloween costume. He is absolutely obsessed with candy, but he would also be very satisfied with a meal of potatoes and molasses.
